Mission goal
============

Lady Squirrel ask you to join her in the garden.
When you arrive Lady Squirrel asks you for the absolute path to the flower garden so she will never be lost,
and could always  reach the beautiful flower garden from anywhere.

Run gsh check

Useful commands
===============

pwd
  See the path to your current location.


Ctrl-S-c/Ctrl-S-v
   In a terminal you can use the combine touch "control", "shift" and "c"
   to copy a string into the clipboard
   and  the combine touch "control", "shift" and "c" to paste from the clipboard.
